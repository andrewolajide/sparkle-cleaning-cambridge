FAQ

Questions Answered

How do I schedule a cleaning service?

Booking is effortless. Use our online booking form, call us directly, or speak with our AI assistant. Simply select your service, preferred date and time, and we handle everything else.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve the entire metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs within a 30-mile radius. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Do you provide cleaning supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer specific products, we are happy to accommodate your preferences.

Are your cleaners insured and vetted?

Absolutely. All team members undergo thorough background checks and are fully insured and bonded. Your safety and peace of mind are our top priorities.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the slot to other clients and maintain efficient scheduling for our team.
